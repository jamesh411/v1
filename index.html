ABOUT

Currently, I'm a sophomore studying at UC Berkeley. I'm pursuing a BS degree in Bioengineering, concentrating on Synthetic and Computational Biology. In addition, I'm working towards the Berkeley Certificate in Design Innovation.

I love how nature is so dynamic—it produces novel solutions to pressing matters—and that’s why I find plants and their adaptations so fascinating. In the future, I hope to engineer solutions that are inspired by nature, with a dash of human-centered design, to produce bioinspired products that are both visually appealing and useful.
