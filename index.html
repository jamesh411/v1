ABOUT

Currently, I'm a sophomore studying at UC Berkeley. I'm pursuing a BS degree in Bioengineering, concentrating on Synthetic and Computational Biology. In addition, I'm working towards a certificate in Design Innovation.

I love how nature is dynamic—it produces novel solutions to pressing matters—and that’s why I find plants and their adaptations so fascinating. In the future, I hope to engineer solutions that are inspired by nature, with a dash of human-centered design, to produce bioinspired products that are both visually appealing and functional.

SPLOTCHES!

BACK

Game Design

A one-player maze game written in Java. To reach the finish line, players must pick up color splotches along the maze that allow them to change colors and pass through certain walls.

As part of a 4 person team, I created the graphics for the user interface and game play. In addition, I also worked on basic game mechanics by employing object-oriented design, data structures, GUI and Java Swing classes. Original idea and level design created by Evan Charfauros. Game created 2017.

Skill: Java, Adobe Illustrator
