ABOUT

GET TO KNOW ME

Currently, I'm a sophomore at UC Berkeley pursuing a BS degree in Bioengineering, and concentrating on Synthetic and Computational Biology. Also, I'm working towards a certificate in Design Innovation.

I'm captivated by nature: it is dynamic and produces novel solutions to pressing matters. In the future, I hope to engineer solutions that are inspired by plants, with a dash of design, to produce bioinspired devices that are both visually appealing and functional.

I can tell you, with certainty, that I love foliage and lychee more than the average person should, but that's okay! I'm also allergic to the cold, and walk fast enough that I regularly cut time estimates in half.

APPAREL & GRAPHIC DESIGN

A portfolio of my work for EthiCAL Apparel that includes designs for our line, marketing campaigns and custom orders.

EthiCAL is a socially and sustainably driven student-run business that sells apparel on campus and provides custom order service. In addition, we are also a social enterprise that focuses on lending our profits as microloans via Kiva, a nonprofit microfinance platform. We look to support entrepreneurs on campus and across the globe.

Find out more about us here.
View my products here.

Skill: Adobe Illustrator

LINE - PINS, STICKERS AND T-SHIRTS DESIGNED TO SELL ON CAMPUS

These final products were the result of weeks of ideation, sketches, feedback and mock-ups. Each design was carefully made, taking into consideration our audience, aesthetics and innovation. I then experimented with each final product by trying out new mediums of printing. The Banana Leaf and Go Bears designs are both enamel pins—something we had not previously sold. The Eggsplore! design is embroidered on the left-breast of a t-shirt and has sold 150+ units to date. Lastly, the Fruit Labels design, my most recent, is a sticker sheet printed with a matte coating.

MARKETING - BANNERS CREATED TO MARKET SALES EVENTS

These were used as promotional material on our social media accounts to generate interest in our weekly sales events. I designed them to be both eye-catching and informative on our mission and the logistics of that specific sales event.

CUSTOM ORDERS - STICKER AND T-SHIRTS CRAFTED FOR CLIENTS

SPLOTCHES!

BACK

GAME DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

A one-player maze game written in Java. To reach the finish line, players must pick up color splotches along the maze that allow them to change colors and pass through certain walls.

As part of a 4 person team, I created the graphics for the user interface and game play. In addition, I also worked on basic game mechanics by employing object-oriented design, data structures, GUI and Java Swing classes. Original idea and level design created by Evan Charfauros. Game created 2017.

Skill: Java, Adobe Illustrator
